## see_in_dark var (mob)

<!-- -->    
**Default value:**    
:   2    
This determines how far the mob can see in the dark. The scale is just    
like luminosity: a value of 1 illuminates the mob and its location; 2    
illuminates the immediate surrounds; and so on.
