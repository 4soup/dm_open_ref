## Turn proc (matrix)

+   [matrix](/ref/matrix.md)

+   [turn proc (applied to a matrix)](/ref/proc/turn/matrix.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   Turn(angle)
<!-- -->
**Args:**
+   angle: The angle of clockwise rotation, in degrees
<!-- -->

+   src
