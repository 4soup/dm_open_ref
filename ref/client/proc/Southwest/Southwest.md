## Southwest proc (client)    
**See also:**    
:   [Move proc (client)](/ref/client/proc/Move.md)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   Southwest()    
<!-- -->    
**Returns:**    
:   Returns 1 on success; 0 on failure.    
<!-- -->    
**When:**    
:   Called when the player presses the \"down-left\" key or cursor.    
<!-- -->    
**Default action:**    
:   Calls src.Move() towards the southwest.
