## East proc (client)

**Format:**
+   East()
<!-- -->
**Returns:**
+   1 on success; 0 on failure.
<!-- -->
**When:**
+   Called when the player presses the \"right\" key or cursor.
<!-- -->
**Default action:**
+   Calls src.Move() towards the east.

**See also:**
+   [Move proc (client)](/ref/client/proc/Move.md) <!-- -->
