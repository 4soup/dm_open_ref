## North proc (client)    
**See also:**    
:   [Move proc (client)](/ref/client/proc/Move/Move.md)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   North()    
<!-- -->    
**Returns:**    
:   1 on success; 0 on failure.    
<!-- -->    
**When:**    
:   Called when the player presses the \"up\" key or cursor.    
<!-- -->    
**Default action:**    
:   Calls src.Move() towards the north.
