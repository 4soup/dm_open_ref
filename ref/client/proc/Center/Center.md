## Center proc (client)    
**See also:**    
:   [walk proc](/ref/proc/walk)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   Center()    
<!-- -->    
**When:**    
:   Called when the player presses the \"center\" key or cursor.    
<!-- -->    
**Default action:**    
:   Cancels any automated movement by calling walk(usr,0).
