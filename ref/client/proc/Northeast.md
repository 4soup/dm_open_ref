## Northeast proc (client)
**See also:**
*   [Move proc (client)](/ref/client/proc/Move.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
*   Northeast()
<!-- -->
**Returns:**
*   1 on success; 0 on failure.
<!-- -->
**When:**
*   Called when the player presses the \"up-right\" key or cursor.
<!-- -->
**Default action:**
*   Calls src.Move() towards the northeast.
