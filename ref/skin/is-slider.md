## is-slider parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [Bar](/ref/skin/control/bar.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   false


Make this an adjustable slider capable of being changed by the
user, instead of a progress bar.

> [!TIP] 
> **See also:**
> +   [value parameter](/ref/skin/param/value.md)
