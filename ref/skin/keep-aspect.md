## keep-aspect parameter (skin)


**Applies to:**
+   [Label](/ref/skin/control/label.md) 
+   [Main](/ref/skin/control/main.md) 
**Format:**
+   true/false

**Default value:**
+   false


If stretching a background image, preserve its aspect ratio.
