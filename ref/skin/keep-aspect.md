## keep-aspect parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [Label](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/label.md) 
+   [Main](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/main.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   false


If stretching a background image, preserve its aspect ratio.
