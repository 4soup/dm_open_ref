## small-icons parameter (skin)


**Applies to:**
+   [Grid](/ref/skin/control/grid.md) 
**Format:**
+   true/false

**Default value:**
+   false


When output(object,grid) is sent, show smaller icons in this
control instead of larger ones.

> [!TIP] 
> **See also:**
> +   [show-names parameter](/ref/skin/param/show-names.md)
