## small-icons parameter (skin)    
**See also:**    
:   [show-names parameter](/%7Bskin%7D/param/show-names)    
<!-- -->    
**Applies to:**    
:   [Grid](/%7Bskin%7D/control/grid)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   true/false    
<!-- -->    
**Default value:**    
:   false    
When output(object,grid) is sent, show smaller icons in this control    
instead of larger ones.
