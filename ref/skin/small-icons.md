  ## small-icons parameter (skin)
  **See also:**
  :   [show-names parameter](ref/%7Bskin%7D/param/show-names)
  <!-- -->
  **Applies to:**
  :   [Grid](ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/grid)
  <!-- -->
  **Format:**
  :   true/false
  <!-- -->
  **Default value:**
  :   false
  When output(object,grid) is sent, show smaller icons in this control
  instead of larger ones.
