## screen-pos parameter (skin) 
###### BYOND Version 515


**Applies to:**
+   [Main](/ref/skin/control/main.md) 
**Format:**
+   *x*,*y*


Read-only. 

For windows, this is the upper left corner
of the nearest monitor\'s area.
