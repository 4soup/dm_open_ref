## statusbar parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [Main](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/main.md) (window only)
<!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   true


Shows a status bar at the bottom of the window. This will show
the name of an atom when you hover over it with the mouse.

> **See also:**
