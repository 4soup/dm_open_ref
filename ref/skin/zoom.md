## zoom parameter (skin)


**Applies to:**
+   [Map](/ref/skin/control/map.md) 
**Format:**
+   number

**Default value:**
+   0


Zoom factor for icons on the map. 1 means to show the icons at
their original size, 2 is 200%, 0.5 is 50%, and so on. A value of 0
stretches to fit available space.
