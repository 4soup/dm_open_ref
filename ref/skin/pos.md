## pos parameter (skin)

*   [anchor1, anchor2 parameters](/%7Bskin%7D/param/anchor)
*   [size parameter](/%7Bskin%7D/param/size)

**Applies to:**
*   [All](/%7Bskin%7D/control)
<!-- -->
**Format:**
*   *x*,*y*

**Default value:**
*   *x*,*y*

container. (Not applicable to panes.)
