## show-splitter parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [Child](/ref/skin/control/child.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   true


Show a splitter if both the left and right (or top and bottom)
panes are in use. The splitter can be dragged to resize the panes.
