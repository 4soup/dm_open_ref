## max-lines parameter (skin)
**Applies to:**
+   [Output](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/output.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   number
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   1000


Maximum number of lines before the control drops old text to
make room for more. 0 is no limit. 

An overflow of 5% is
allowed, to reduce flicker.
