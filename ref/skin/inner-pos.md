**Applies to:**    
:   [Main](ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/main)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   *x*,*y*    
Read-only.    
Reads the position where the window\'s interior contents begin (i.e.,    
not counting titlebar, statusbar, borders, etc.), relative to its    
`outer-pos`.
