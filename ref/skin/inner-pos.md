## inner-pos parameter (skin) 
###### BYOND Version 515


**Applies to:**
+   [Main](/ref/skin/control/main.md) 
**Format:**
+   *x*,*y*


Read-only. 

Reads the position where the window\'s
interior contents begin (i.e., not counting titlebar, statusbar,
borders, etc.), relative to its `outer-pos`.
