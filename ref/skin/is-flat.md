## is-flat parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [Button](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/button.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   false


Gives this button a flat appearance instead of pseudo-3D
highlights.

**See also:**
+   [button-type parameter](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/param/button-type.md)
