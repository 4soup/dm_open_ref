## is-flat parameter (skin)


**Applies to:**
+   [Button](/ref/skin/control/button.md) 
**Format:**
+   true/false

**Default value:**
+   false


Gives this button a flat appearance instead of pseudo-3D
highlights.

> [!TIP] 
> **See also:**
> +   [button-type parameter](/ref/skin/param/button-type.md)
