## is-flat parameter (skin)
**See also:**
*   [button-type parameter](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/param/button-type.md) -m
<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
*   [Button](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/button.md) -m<!-- -->
**Format:**
*   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
*   false


Gives this button a flat appearance instead of pseudo-3D
highlights.
