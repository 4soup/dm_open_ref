## is-flat parameter (skin)    
**See also:**    
:   [button-type parameter](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/param/button-type)    
<!-- -->    
**Applies to:**    
:   [Button](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/button)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   true/false    
<!-- -->    
**Default value:**    
:   false    
Gives this button a flat appearance instead of pseudo-3D highlights.
