## is-disabled parameter (skin)

<!-- -->
**Applies to:**
+   [All](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control.md) 
+   [Macro](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/macro.md) 
+   [Menu](/ref/%7Bskin%7D/control/menu.md) <!-- -->
**Format:**
+   true/false
<!-- -->
**Default value:**
+   false


Disables the control, menu item, or macro.
