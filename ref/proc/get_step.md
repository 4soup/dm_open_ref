## get_step proc

**Format:**
+   get_step(Ref, Dir)
<!-- -->
**Returns:**
+   The location of the new position.
<!-- -->
**Args:**
+   Ref: Starting point or object.
+   Dir: One of `NORTH`, `SOUTH`, `EAST`, `WEST`, `NORTHEAST`,
    `NORTHWEST`, `SOUTHEAST`, `SOUTHWEST`.


Calculate the position of a step from `Ref` in the direction
`Dir`.
