<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   floor(A)    
<!-- -->    
**Returns:**    
:   the floor of A    
<!-- -->    
**Args:**    
:   A: A number, pixloc, or vector.    
Returns the floor of A (the largest integer less than or equal to A).    
### Example:    
usr \<\< floor(1.45) // outputs 1 usr \<\< floor(-1.45) // outputs -2
