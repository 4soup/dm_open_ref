**Format:**    
:   get_step_rand(Ref)    
<!-- -->    
**Returns:**    
:   The location of the new position, or 0 if no change.    
<!-- -->    
**Args:**    
:   Ref: Starting point or object.    
Calculate position of a step from `Ref` in random motion.
