## volume var (sound)    
**See also:**    
:   [sound proc](/ref/proc/sound/sound.md)    
:   [vars (sound)](/ref/sound/var/var.md)    
<!-- -->    
**Default value:**    
:   100 (full volume)    
Set to a percentage from 0 to 100 of the sound\'s full volume. It will    
play at that volume when it is sent to a player.
