## #undef directive    
**See also:**    
:   [#define directive](/ref/DM/preprocessor/define)    
:   [preprocessor](/ref/DM/preprocessor)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   #undef Name    
<!-- -->    
**Args:**    
:   Name: A macro definition.    
The `#undef` statement removes a macro definition.
