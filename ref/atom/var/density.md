## density var (atom)
**See also:**
*   [Enter proc (atom)](/ref/atom/proc/Enter.md) -m
*   [Entered proc (atom)](/ref/atom/proc/Entered.md) -m
*   [Exit proc (atom)](/ref/atom/proc/Exit.md) -m
*   [Exited proc (atom)](/ref/atom/proc/Exited.md) -m<!-- -->
**Default value:**
*   0 (1 for mobs)


This turns the object\'s density on or off (1 or 0). Two dense
objects may not occupy the same space in the standard movement system.
