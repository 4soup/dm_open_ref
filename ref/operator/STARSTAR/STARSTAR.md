## \*\* operator    
**See also:**    
:   [log proc](/ref/proc/log/log.md)    
:   [operators](/ref/operator/operator.md)    
<!-- -->    
**Format:**    
:   A \*\* B    
<!-- -->    
**Returns:**    
:   A to the power of B.    
### Example:    
usr \<\< 2 \*\* 3 // outputs 8
